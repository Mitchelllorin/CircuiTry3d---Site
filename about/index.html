About CircuiTry3D

Born from a belief that electricity is too abstract to learn from a textbook alone — we built the tool we wished we had.

CircuiTry3D was created to solve a fundamental problem in electrical education: students read formulas, draw diagrams, and still don’t truly see what electricity is doing. We set out to fix that.

By combining real-time 3D simulation with multi-scale electron visualization — from macro wire glow all the way to quantum probability clouds — CircuiTry3D makes invisible forces visible and tangible for learners of every level.

From the Free Sandbox that requires zero sign-up, to a full two-year post-secondary curriculum, to Classroom Mode for teachers managing entire rosters — every feature serves one mission: make electricity click.

CircuiTry3D is independently developed with a deep commitment to accessibility, education, and pushing the boundaries of what a learning app can be.
