Data Safety

Effective Date: October 25, 2025  ·  Last Updated: April 12, 2026

This page explains what data CircuiTry3D collects, why it is collected, how it is handled, and your rights regarding that data. It mirrors the disclosures made in our Google Play Data Safety section and is kept in sync across our app, site, and repository.

Data Collection Summary

The table below summarizes every category of data that CircuiTry3D may collect.

Data Type Collected Purpose Shared with Third Parties Encrypted in Transit User Can Request Deletion
Email address Yes Account registration, authentication, password recovery, service updates No Yes Yes
Purchase history & transaction IDs Yes Subscription management, customer support No (processed by Google Play Billing) Yes Yes
App usage data & circuit designs Yes (account users) Saving projects, feature improvement, usage analytics No Yes Yes
Authentication tokens Yes Maintaining login state No Yes Yes — on logout or account deletion
Classroom roster data (teachers only) Yes — opt-in Classroom Mode sync via Upstash Redis No Yes Yes
Diagnostic & crash data Yes — anonymized App stability and bug fixes No Yes No (anonymized; cannot be linked to a user)

Free Sandbox (No Account Required)

The Free Sandbox mode requires no account and collects no personal information. All circuit designs are saved locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

Data Sharing

CircuiTry3D does not sell your personal data to third parties. Data may be processed by the following service providers solely to operate the app:

  • Google Play Billing — payment processing for in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Upstash Redis — cloud sync for Classroom Mode (only when that feature is enabled by a teacher)
  • Anonymized analytics — aggregated, non-identifiable usage metrics to improve app performance

Each provider is bound by their own data processing agreements and privacy policies.

Data Security

  • All personal data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely using industry-standard practices
  • We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data
  • Access to user data is restricted to authorized personnel only

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time through the /delete-account flow inside the app or by contacting us directly.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability — export your circuit designs and app data
  • Opt-out — opt out of non-essential data collection

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: circuitry3dsim@gmail.com

Children’s Data

CircuiTry3D is designed for educational purposes and is appropriate for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. Classroom Mode operates under teacher supervision, and student data is processed only as directed by the educator. Contact us immediately at circuitry3dsim@gmail.com if you believe we have inadvertently collected a child’s data.

Compliance

Our data practices are designed to comply with:

  • Google Play Developer Program Policies & Data Safety requirements
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA) — for Classroom Mode
